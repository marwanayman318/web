About Trapped Egypt

Trapped Egypt is an immersive entertainment experience located in the heart of Cairo, designed to transport players into the depths of ancient mystery. From the dusty tombs of forgotten pharaohs to the glittering secrets of Cleopatra's court, our escape rooms are crafted with detailed storytelling, atmospheric effects, and intricate puzzles.

Each escape room is built by a team of game designers, historians, and set builders to ensure authenticity and excitement. Whether you're a group of friends, coworkers, tourists, or students, Trapped Egypt provides a thrilling adventure that challenges your logic, teamwork, and courage.
