Sands of Time Room

Story:An ancient hourglass that controls time itself has been shattered, and reality is unraveling. You’re caught in a time loop that sends you jumping between ancient Egypt, the Roman Empire, and a dystopian future. Each puzzle you solve restores part of the hourglass—but time is running out fast.

Objective: Reassemble the shattered Hourglass of Time across different timelines.
Solve chronological riddles in historical and futuristic settings.
Escape the time loop before it resets permanently.

FeaturesTime-jumping puzzles with changing room environments
Sand-based pressure triggers
Clockwork gears and interactive timeline wall

  • Duration: 60 Minutes
  • Players: 3–6
  • Theme: Fantasy + Time Travel
