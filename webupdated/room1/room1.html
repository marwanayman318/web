Pharaoh's Curse

Story: You and your team are archaeologists who unearthed the tomb of a forgotten Pharaoh. As you examine the artifacts, the chamber door slams shut. A hieroglyph begins to glow—the Pharaoh’s curse has been activated. Legend says no one who broke the seal lived to tell the tale. You must decipher sacred texts, solve cryptic puzzles, and break the curse before your souls become part of the tomb forever.

Objective: Find the Sarcophagus Key, solve 7 sacred trials, and escape within 60 minutes.

Features:Ancient Egyptian symbols & artifacts
Hidden doors activated by pressure plates
Atmospheric lighting and eerie sound design

  • Difficulty: Hard
  • Duration: 60 Minutes
  • Players: 3–6
  • Theme: Horror + Mystery
  • Recommended Age: 14+
